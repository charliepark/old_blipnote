About

Blipnote is simple.

During your days and nights on the web, you want to capture short notes, ideas, links and other ephemera so you can process them later. Your email makes a great inbox for those notes. But it’s harder than it should be to just
send yourself an email.

Blipnote fixes that, and lets you
get on with your life. Yay.

It’s free, and run by Charlie Park. Questions? hello@blipnote.com.

It’d be so, so cool of you to
share Blipnote on Twitter.
